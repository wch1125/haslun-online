▸ PAINT BAY

Ship livery customization using watercolor color theory

How It Works

This system uses watercolor glazing theory to generate harmonious color palettes. Unlike simple RGB mixing, glazing simulates how transparent pigment layers interact when painted over each other—the way real watercolors work.

When you select 2-3 base colors, the algorithm calculates glaze combinations: Color A over B, B over A, and (with 3 colors) all pairwise and triple combinations. This produces natural-looking palettes that work well together because they're derived from simulated physical color mixing.

The resulting palette is then mapped to your ship sprite by analyzing its original colors and replacing them based on brightness—shadows stay dark, highlights stay light, but now using your custom color scheme.
